Welcome to Aryabhata Paramedical College!

Shiradi Sai Education Trust (R) Aryabhata Paramedical College provide quality knowledge and training in the field of Para Medical and allied Medical sciences at the grass root level, and provide its students vocational knowledge, that is job oriented, so that the students are ahead of others without wasting their precious time and money in academic courses, which are not so relevant in today's competitive environment.

Our College puts stress on both quality and quantity supported by our dedicated faculty & guest lectures, Seminars and workshops are arranged frequently by eminent personalities of the industry. The students benefit directly from the professional environment in which they study and live.

  • Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology

    Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology - DMLT Medical Lab Technician Diploma course. Medical laboratory technology is the branch of medical science responsible for performing laboratory investigations relating to the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disease.

    SSLC 3yr PUC(PCMB) 2yr Intake: 20 Seats
    Subscribe Course

  • Diploma in Dialysis Technology

    Our institutions offer a 3-year Diploma programme in dialysis technology. The Diploma in Dialysis Technician, also known as Diploma in Dialysis Technology (DDT) is a paramedical course aimed at training students in operating Dialysis machines and more.

    SSLC 3yr PUC(PCMB) 2yr Intake: 20 Seats
    Subscribe Course

  • Diploma in Health Inspector

    DHI is Paramedical professionals hold an important place in the healthcare sector. Although the real decision making lies in the hands of doctors and surgeons, paramedical professionals make sure that the guidelines suggested by doctors are implemented in a proper manner.

    SSLC 3yr PUC(PCMB) 2yr Intake: 20 Seats
    Subscribe Course

  • Diploma in Medical Imaging Technology

    Advance Diploma in Medical Imaging Technology is a Diploma level Radiography program. Medical Imaging Technology is a study of the techniques to perform Imaging of the human beings in Radiology Department like X-ray, CT scan, MRI etc. for diagnosis of Diseases.

    SSLC 3yr PUC(PCMB) 2yr Intake: 20 Seats
    Subscribe Course

  • Diploma in Ophthalmic Technology

    Diploma in ophthalmic technology also known as DOT is a two-year diploma course which is designed to produce trained and qualified professionals who assist ophthalmologists in order to diagnose and treat patients. After the completion of the DOT course, students will have a solid foundation in eye care.

    SSLC 3yr PUC(PCMB) 2yr Intake: 20 Seats
    Subscribe Course
