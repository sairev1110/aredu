Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology - DMLT

Duration : 2 OR 3 YEARS COURSE

Eligibility : SSLC OR PUC (SCIENCE)

Intake : 20 SEATS

Apply Now

Course Duration:

3 years Course Plus 3 months internship for S.S.L.C / X std students
Or 2 years Course Plus 3 months internship for II PUC (science) / XII students.

Course Description :


Diploma in The Medical Lab Technician (DMLT) course at Aryabhata Paramedical College prepares students for rewarding in-demand career opportunities. The course deals with prognosis, diagnosis, and follow-up through the use of clinical laboratory tests. This professionally woven and well-integrated course provides an exhaustive learning experience and aims towards a basic understanding of varied aspects of lab and diagnostic techniques.


During the medical laboratory technician course, students learn different test procedures to diagnose and detect diseases, ultimately paving way for the doctors to treat their patients. In addition to it, the curriculum deals with collection of patient information, sampling, reporting, testing and documentation. Students learn about Hematology, Cytopathology, Histopathology, Immuno his to chemistry, Molecular Pathology, Biochemistry etc. and receive hands-on training to work as a full-fledged lab technologists at our constituent hospital.


The lab technician course covers elements of blood bank management, materials management, supply chain management as well as lab information system management. We ensure compliance to quality control standards as per the regulations of NABL and students are also taught how to clean and maintain the lab equipment along with proper management of biomedical waste.


Aryabhata Paramedical College innovative teaching methodologies are at par with the best College in Bengaluru. Regular sessions on communication and personality development are conducted to bridge the gap between the employability needs and other desired attributes, so that our students get good placements with organizations of repute.



Programme Objective :


To educate and train students about sample collection techniques for routine and specialized tests, and thereafter conduct the required diagnostic tests, followed with an analysis of the outcome.


Course aims to inculcate the required skills and knowledge during the course study:


  • Comprehension of Medical Terminology.
  • Laboratory Procedures (theory and applied learning components)and quality control.
  • Pathology Lab Orientation with requisite knowledge of laboratory equipment.
  • Handling, maintenance and disinfection of medical laboratory equipment.
  • Training in the blood bank and basic hematology and clinical biochemistry.
  • Blood grouping & collection procedures.


Curriculam of the Course :


DMLT 2nd YEAR :

  • Paper-I : ANATOMY & PHYSIOLOGY
  • Paper-II : BIOCHEMISTRY
  • Paper-III : MICROBIOLOGY
  • Paper-IV : ANATOMY & PHYSIOLOGY

DMLT 3rd YEAR :

  • Paper-I : BIOCHEMISTRY
  • Paper-II : MICROBIOLOGY
  • Paper-III : ANATOMY & PHYSIOLOGY
